# Psychologist — A Tier Class

A high risk, high reward pick. Doubled Sanity effects mean you recover faster from positive sources but also take more damage from negative ones. Level 2+ adds a chance to ignore Sanity loss entirely.

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Cost** | 500 Animal Coins |
| **Tier** | A |
| **Role** | Support / Risk-Reward Sanity Management |
| **Rarity** | Standard |
| Sanity | Variable (high risk/high reward) |
| Utility | Medium |
| Starter_Money | None |


## Core Ability

All Sanity effects are doubled (positive AND negative)

## Upgrade Levels

### Level 1
All Sanity effects are doubled (positive and negative)

### Level 2
All Sanity effects are doubled (positive and negative), with a 7.5% chance to ignore Sanity loss

### Level 3
All Sanity effects are doubled (positive and negative), with a 15% chance to ignore Sanity loss

## Strategy Tips







High risk, high reward. Pairs incredibly well with Sanity recovery items like Coffee and Chocolate. The doubled effects can make you nearly invincible — or destroy you if you're not careful.
