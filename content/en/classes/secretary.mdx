# Secretary — B Tier Class

The best class for front desk duty, the Secretary turns the normally Sanity-draining task of checking in patients into a self-sustaining workflow. At 120 Animal Coins, it is one of the most cost-effective B Tier unlocks available.

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Cost** | 120 Animal Coins |
| **Tier** | B |
| **Role** | Front Desk / Support |
| **Rarity** | Standard |
| Sanity | Medium-High |
| Utility | High (Reception) |

## Core Ability

Recover 1 Sanity when checking in patients — every successful check-in at the reception desk restores 1 Sanity point, counteracting the usual drain from repetitive tasks.

## Upgrade Levels

### Level 1
Recover 1 Sanity when checking in patients

### Level 2
Recover 1 Sanity when checking in patients, +5 Bonus Sanity

### Level 3
Recover 1 Sanity when checking in patients, +10 Bonus Sanity

## Strategy Tips

The Secretary excels in any game where you plan to spend the majority of your time at the front desk. Checking in patient after patient normally grinds down your Sanity quickly, but the Secretary's per-check-in recovery turns this into a zero-sum or even positive loop at Level 3. Pair this class with a team that has dedicated Doctors and Surgeons so you can focus entirely on reception without worrying about treatment.

At Level 2, the +5 Bonus Sanity gives you breathing room during the opening minutes of a shift before check-ins start flowing. Level 3's +10 Bonus Sanity is the sweet spot — combined with per-check-in recovery, you can run the front desk indefinitely without ever needing a break. If your team lacks a Secretary, patients pile up at reception and the whole hospital slows down, making this class surprisingly impactful for team efficiency.

## Comparison to Adjacent Tier Classes

| Class | Cost | Key Benefit |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Nurse** | 10 Coins | +1 Inventory Slot |
| **Secretary** | 120 Coins | Sanity on Check-In |
| **Doctor** | 900 Coins | Sanity on Heal |

The Secretary costs only 120 coins yet provides B Tier utility — an incredible value. The Doctor costs nearly eight times as much for a similar Sanity-on-action mechanic, though the Doctor's healing role is generally more versatile than reception duty. Compared to the Nurse, the Secretary is a massive leap in effectiveness for just 110 extra coins. If you enjoy front desk management, the Secretary is the single best value upgrade in the game.
