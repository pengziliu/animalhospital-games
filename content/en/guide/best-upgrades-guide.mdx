# Best Upgrades Guide — Priority & Cost-Benefit Analysis

Shop upgrades in Animal Hospital provide **permanent passive bonuses** that improve every future shift. With limited in-game dollars, knowing which upgrades to buy first — and which to skip — is essential for climbing the class progression efficiently.

This guide ranks every upgrade by value, breaks down the cost-benefit of each, and provides budget roadmaps for both free-to-play and paid players.

## Cost-Benefit Analysis

### ⭐⭐⭐ 50% Faster DNA Analysis — $110

**Effect**: Cuts DNA analysis time from approximately **9 seconds to 4.5 seconds**.

| Metric | Before | After | Savings |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| DNA Analysis Time | ~9 seconds | ~4.5 seconds | 4.5 seconds per patient |

**Why it's top priority**: DNA analysis is one of the most frequent actions during a shift. Saving 4.5 seconds per patient compounds rapidly — over a full shift with 20+ patients, that's **90+ seconds saved**. Time saved = more patients treated = more Animal Coins earned.

**Verdict**: **Buy first**. The best $110 you'll spend.

---

### ⭐⭐⭐ Patients Recover 25% Faster — $115

**Effect**: Reduces patient recovery time from approximately **35 seconds to 26 seconds**.

| Metric | Before | After | Savings |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| Recovery Time | ~35 seconds | ~26 seconds | 9 seconds per patient |

**Why it's top priority**: Faster recovery means patients cycle through treatment rooms quicker. This increases patient throughput and reduces bottlenecks during busy shifts. Also indirectly helps with Sanity — healed patients create less pressure on the team.

**Verdict**: **Buy second**. Pairs perfectly with Faster DNA Analysis for maximum efficiency.

---

### ⭐⭐⭐ Extra Check-In Window — $190–$200

**Effect**: Adds a **second check-in window**, allowing two patients to be processed simultaneously.

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Check-In Capacity | 1 patient at a time | 2 patients at a time | **2× throughput at reception** |

**Why it's top priority**: This is the single biggest throughput upgrade in the game. A second window means one player can screen while another processes. During high-traffic shifts, this prevents the patient queue from backing up, which is when anomalies slip through undetected.

**Verdict**: **Buy third**. More expensive but transformative for team play. Solo players should deprioritize this.

---

### ⭐⭐ +10% NPC Speed — $150

**Effect**: Patients move **10% faster** through the clinic.

**Why it's good**: Faster patient movement reduces bottlenecks between the waiting room and treatment areas. Patients reach treatment rooms quicker, which means faster cycling.

**Why it's not top tier**: The 10% speed increase is noticeable but not dramatic. It helps smooth out the patient flow but doesn't directly increase your screening or healing efficiency.

**Verdict**: **Buy fourth** if you have spare dollars. Good quality-of-life improvement.

---

### ⭐⭐ 33% Faster Computers — $110

**Effect**: Reduces room computer interaction time from approximately **2 seconds to 1.3 seconds**.

| Metric | Before | After | Savings |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| Computer Time | ~2 seconds | ~1.3 seconds | 0.7 seconds per use |

**Why it's good**: Room computers are used frequently for patient processing. The time savings add up over a shift, and the low $110 cost makes it accessible.

**Why it's not top tier**: 0.7 seconds saved per interaction is meaningful but much less impactful than the DNA Analysis or Recovery upgrades. Computers are used less frequently than DNA analysis.

**Verdict**: **Buy fifth**. Good value for $110 but lower priority than the top three.

---

### ⭐ Visitors Check In Faster — $20

**Effect**: Removes the short **hesitation delay** before patients begin check-in.

**Why it's situational**: At only $20, it's cheap. But the time saved per patient is minimal — a fraction of a second. The impact on overall shift efficiency is negligible compared to other upgrades.

**Verdict**: **Skip or buy last**. Only worth it if you have $20 burning a hole in your pocket.

---

### ⭐ +12% NPC Speed — Varies

**Effect**: Patients move **12% faster** through the clinic.

**Why it's situational**: This overlaps with the +10% NPC Speed upgrade. The marginal improvement of an additional 2% is not worth spending limited resources on.

**Verdict**: **Skip**. Redundant with +10% NPC Speed.

## Purchase Priority Roadmap

### Free-to-Play Budget Plan

Total recommended investment: **$415–$425**

| Priority | Upgrade | Cost | Cumulative |
|----------|---------|------|-----------|
| 1st | 50% Faster DNA Analysis | $110 | $110 |
| 2nd | Patients Recover 25% Faster | $115 | $225 |
| 3rd | Extra Check-In Window | $190–$200 | $415–$425 |

These three upgrades give you maximum shift efficiency for under $425. Everything after this is optional.

### Extended Free-to-Play Plan

If you want to buy everything useful:

| Priority | Upgrade | Cost | Cumulative |
|----------|---------|------|-----------|
| 4th | +10% NPC Speed | $150 | $565–$575 |
| 5th | 33% Faster Computers | $110 | $675–$685 |

### Robux Player Plan

If you're spending Robux, your priorities shift:

1. **Secret Agent class** (320–890 R$) — gives you a Gun, the most powerful tool in the game
2. **Head Nurse class** (80–190 R$) — +3 inventory for carrying everything you need
3. **Chocolate** (29 R$) — emergency Sanity recovery in your pocket

After these, use in-game dollars for the upgrade roadmap above.

## Dollar Earning Tips for Upgrades

Need dollars faster to buy upgrades? Here's how:

- ✅ **Complete every patient** — untreated patients = no dollars
- ✅ **Play longer shifts** — more time = more patients = more dollars
- ✅ **Use efficient classes** — Surgeon and Doctor heal faster, cycling patients quicker
- ✅ **Avoid Sanity drain** — dying mid-shift cuts your earnings short
- ✅ **Buy consumables wisely** — skip items you won't use; save for permanent upgrades

---

> 💡 **Golden Rule**: Prioritize **permanent upgrades** over **consumable items**. A $110 DNA upgrade saves you time every single shift forever. A $120 Chocolate is gone after three bites.
