## Purchase Priority Guide

### Free-to-Play Priority
1. Save for **Surgeon class** (2,500 Animal Coins)
2. **50% Faster DNA Analysis** ($110)
3. **Patients Recover 25% Faster** ($115)
4. **Extra Check-In Window** ($190-200)

### Paid Priority
1. **Secret Agent class** (Robux) for Gun access
2. **Chocolate** (29 Robux) for emergency Sanity recovery
3. **Patients Recover 25% Faster** upgrade

## What NOT to Buy

- **Security class** — X-Taser is free in the Reception Office
- **Fire Extinguisher** — Already available for free in the shop
- **Shock People (3 uses)** — Low utility for the cost
- **1-3 Animal Coins** — Terrible value, earn through gameplay
