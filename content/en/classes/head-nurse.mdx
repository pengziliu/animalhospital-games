# Head Nurse — B/S Tier Class

A well-rounded premium utility class that blends inventory expansion, passive Sanity recovery, and item generation. Tier rankings vary by source — IGN places it in S Tier for its versatile kit, while TechWiser ranks it B Tier due to its Robux-only cost and competition from cheaper alternatives.

## Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Cost** | 80 Robux (sources vary: 80–190 Robux) |
| **Tier** | B / S (disputed) |
| **Role** | Support / Utility |
| **Rarity** | Premium (Robux) |
| Sanity | Medium (Level 1), High (Level 2–3) |
| Utility | Very High |

## Core Ability

+3 Max Inventory Capacity — carry three additional items beyond the default limit, a significant logistical advantage for supply management and team support.

## Upgrade Levels

### Level 1
+3 Max Inventory Capacity

### Level 2
+3 Max Inventory Capacity, +10 Bonus Sanity

### Level 3
+3 Max Inventory Capacity, Start each round with all items

## Strategy Tips

The Head Nurse's greatest strength is versatility. The +3 inventory slots at Level 1 already match the Nurse's Level 3 cap, and the class only gets stronger from there. At Level 2, the +10 Bonus Sanity means you can function as a frontline support without constantly retreating to recover. Level 3 is where the class truly shines — starting every round with a full item loadout means zero time wasted scavenging for supplies.

The S Tier argument comes from the Level 3 item generation combined with inventory space, effectively making you a self-sustaining support hub for the entire team. The B Tier argument is simpler: at 80+ Robux, you could argue that spending on Surgeon or Doctor upgrades provides more direct gameplay impact. If you play primarily as a support player who manages team supplies, the Head Nurse is exceptional. If you prefer active treatment roles, consider allocating your Robux elsewhere.

## Comparison to Adjacent Tier Classes

| Class | Cost | Key Benefit |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **Secretary** | 120 Coins | Sanity on Check-In |
| **Head Nurse** | 80 Robux | +3 Inventory + Items |
| **Surgeon** | ~2,000+ Coins | Advanced Healing |

The Head Nurse outclasses the Secretary in raw utility thanks to inventory expansion and item generation, but costs premium currency rather than earnable Animal Coins. Compared to the Surgeon, the Head Nurse offers broader utility while the Surgeon provides specialized surgical healing. The two actually complement each other well — a Head Nurse managing supplies while a Surgeon handles critical patients makes for a devastating team combo.
