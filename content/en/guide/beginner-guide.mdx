# Animal Hospital Beginner Guide

## What is Animal Hospital?

Animal Hospital is a **co-op survival horror game** on Roblox where you play as a receptionist working the night shift at a veterinary clinic. Your job: screen animal patients for hidden anomalies — monsters disguised as ordinary animals.

**Key facts:**
- Free to play on Roblox
- Up to 30 players per server
- Developed by Animal Anomaly (led by Roytt)
- Released May 10, 2026
- 45.2M+ total visits

## The Core Gameplay Loop

Every shift follows the same cycle:

1. **Patients arrive** — Animals appear at your reception window
2. **Screen for anomalies** — Check appearance, photo, and CCTV
3. **Treat patients** — Heal real patients through medical procedures
4. **Survive** — Maintain your Sanity above 0 through the entire shift

## Understanding Sanity

Your **Sanity** is your health bar. If it hits 0, you are eliminated from the shift. Sanity drains from:

- Encountering anomalies and monsters
- Looking at cursed photos (-10 Sanity)
- Being stared at by the Stalker (-10 Sanity)
- Various events and environmental hazards

**How to recover Sanity:**
- Heal patients as Doctor/Surgeon
- Check in patients as Secretary
- Use Coffee (+5 Sanity per sip)
- Use Chocolate (+20 Sanity per bite)

## Three Detection Methods

There are **three ways** to check if a patient is an anomaly:

### 1. Appearance
Look directly at the patient. Some anomalies have visible signs: extra eyes, twitching, creepy smiles, hollow faces.

### 2. Photo
Take a polaroid photo and wait for it to develop on the desk. Some anomalies only show up in the photo — not in person.

### 3. CCTV
Check surveillance cameras, especially the waiting room camera. Some anomalies only appear on camera feeds.

## Your First Shift — Step by Step

1. Start as **Intern** (free class)
2. When a patient arrives, **look at them for 4-5 seconds**
3. **Take a photo** and wait for it to develop on the desk
4. While waiting, **check the waiting room CCTV**
5. If anything looks suspicious, **close the shutters**
6. If everything looks normal, **stamp their details and admit them**
7. **Treat admitted patients** in the treatment rooms
8. **Keep Sanity above 0** using Coffee (free in lobby)

## First Purchases Priority

1. **Doctor** (900 Animal Coins) — Sanity recovery on healing
2. **Surgeon** (2,500 Animal Coins) — Best free class, self-sustaining
3. **50% Faster DNA Analysis** ($110) — Speeds up diagnosis
4. **Patients Recover 25% Faster** ($115) — More throughput

## Essential Tips

- **Never rush the screening** — some anomalies are subtle (twitching, mismatched eyes)
- **Always check CCTV** — some anomalies are invisible in person AND in photos
- **Listen for audio cues** — Skinwalker roars, Hider breathing
- **Keep Coffee in inventory** — it's free and restores Sanity
- **Play with friends** — co-op is much easier than solo
