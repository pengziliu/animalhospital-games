# Animal Coins Guide — How to Earn & Spend Coins Fast

Animal Coins are the **primary progression currency** in Animal Hospital. You need them to unlock powerful classes like Surgeon (2,500 AC) and Psychologist (500 AC). This guide covers the fastest ways to earn coins, the smartest things to spend them on, and how Animal Coins compare to Robux.

---

## What Are Animal Coins?

Animal Coins are **earned in-game** by completing shifts. They are **not purchasable with real money** — the only way to get them is by playing the game.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Currency Type** | Earned in-game (cannot be bought with Robux) |
| **Used For** | Unlocking classes in the lobby Class Shop |
| **Earn Rate** | ~50–200 per shift (varies by class, efficiency, and shift length) |
| **Reset?** | No — Animal Coins persist across sessions |

---

## How to Earn Animal Coins Fast

### 1. Complete Shifts (Primary Method)

Every shift you survive rewards Animal Coins. The reward scales with:

| Factor | Impact on Coins |
|--------|----------------|
| **Shift length** | Longer shifts = more patients = more coins |
| **Patients treated** | More patients treated = higher score = more coins |
| **Anomalies caught** | Successfully identifying anomalies boosts your score |
| **Class used** | Some classes may earn bonus coins through efficiency |
| **Survival** | Completing the full shift (not dying) maximizes rewards |

**Average earnings**: 50–200 Animal Coins per shift depending on the factors above.

### 2. Use High-Efficiency Classes

The faster you process patients, the more coins you earn per unit of time. These classes maximize throughput:

| Class | Cost | Why It Helps Earning |
|-------|------|---------------------|
| **Surgeon** (2,500 AC) | 2,500 AC | Fastest healer — cycles patients rapidly, earns coins back quickly |
| **Doctor** (900 AC) | 900 AC | Sanity on healing keeps you alive longer = longer shifts = more coins |
| **Secretary** (120 AC) | 120 AC | Fast check-in = quicker patient throughput at reception |
| **Paramedic** (250 AC) | 250 AC | Speed Cola = faster movement between tasks |

> 💡 **Tip**: The Surgeon pays for itself. Earning 2,500 coins takes roughly 15–25 shifts as an Intern. Once unlocked, your per-shift earnings may increase due to higher efficiency.

### 3. Maximize Patient Throughput

More patients treated = more coins. Here's how to process patients faster:

| Strategy | Implementation |
|----------|---------------|
| **Buy upgrades** | 50% Faster DNA Analysis ($110) + Patients Recover 25% Faster ($115) accelerate the pipeline |
| **Extra Check-In Window** | Process two patients simultaneously ($190–$200) |
| **Learn anomaly types** | Faster identification = less time wasted on uncertain patients |
| **Memorize the 3-step protocol** | Consistent screening prevents costly mistakes |

### 4. Survive Full Shifts

Dying early cuts your earnings dramatically. To maximize survival:

- ✅ Always carry **Chocolate** (60 Sanity emergency reserve)
- ✅ Grab free **Coffee** between patients
- ✅ Play **Surgeon or Doctor** for self-sustaining Sanity recovery
- ✅ Close shutters on anomalies immediately — don't hesitate
- ✅ Avoid The Stalker's gaze (-10 Sanity per stare)

### 5. Play During Active Hours

Servers with more players tend to generate more patients and more anomalies. Higher player count = more opportunities to earn coins per shift.

Animal Hospital offers two parallel currencies for different purchases:

| Currency | How to Get | What You Can Buy | Best Value |
|----------|-----------|------------------|------------|
| **Animal Coins (AC)** | Earned in-game by playing | 8 classes (Intern, Nurse, Secretary, Paramedic, Psychologist, Doctor, Security, Surgeon) | **Surgeon (2,500 AC)** — best free class |
| **Robux (R$)** | Real money purchase | 2 classes (Head Nurse, Secret Agent), Chocolate (29 R$), Gun (99 R$) | **Secret Agent** — best combat class |

### Free-to-Play vs Paid Power Comparison

| Category | Free-to-Play (AC) | Paid (Robux) |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|

**Key takeaway**: The best healer in the game (Surgeon) is free-to-earn. Robux primarily unlocks **combat power** (Secret Agent's Gun) and **inventory capacity** (Head Nurse). The game is fully viable without spending real money.

## In-Game Dollars vs Animal Coins

Don't confuse the two currencies:

| | Animal Coins (AC) | In-Game Dollars ($) |
|--|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Earned by** | Completing shifts | Treating patients, shift rewards |
| **Persists between sessions?** | ✅ Yes — saved permanently | ❌ No — shift-based only |
| **Used for** | **Permanent class unlocks** | **Upgrades and consumable items** |
| **Strategy** | Save for expensive classes | Spend freely on upgrades |

**Golden Rule**: Save Animal Coins for **permanent class purchases**. Spend in-game dollars on **upgrades and items** each shift.

## Fast-Track Roadmap

Follow this plan to unlock Surgeon as fast as possible:

### Phase 1: Intern (Free) — Shifts 1–10

- Play Intern to learn the game
- Earn ~50–100 AC per shift
- Focus on learning the 3-step anomaly detection protocol
- **Target savings**: 900 AC

### Phase 2: Doctor (900 AC) — Shifts 10–20

- Unlock Doctor immediately at 900 AC
- Self-sustaining Sanity recovery lets you survive longer shifts
- Earn ~75–150 AC per shift with improved survival
- **Target savings**: 2,500 AC (total needed: 1,600 more)

### Phase 3: Surgeon (2,500 AC total) — Shifts 20–40

- Unlock Surgeon for maximum healing power
- Level up to 3 for doubled Sanity gain
- Earn ~100–200 AC per shift at peak efficiency
- **You've reached endgame** — everything else is optional

### Phase 4: Optional Upgrades

- **Psychologist** (500 AC) if you want 2× Sanity synergy
- **Paramedic** (250 AC) for speed-based playstyle
- Focus on upgrading Surgeon to Level 3
