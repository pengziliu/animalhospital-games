Beyond anomalies, the hospital is home to **dangerous monsters and environmental hazards** that can kill you, drain your Sanity, or destroy equipment. Knowing what each one does and how to respond is essential for survival.

## Detailed Breakdown

### 1. Bed Monster

A creature that **hides under hospital beds**. You'll see a red line or glow beneath the bed indicating its presence.

- **Danger Level:** 🔴 High
- **How to handle:** Give the patient **Maple Syrup**. This calms the Bed Monster and makes it retreat.
- **Warning:** Ignoring the Bed Monster can lead to it attacking you or the patient.
- **Tip:** Always check under beds when entering a room. The red line is your early warning system.

### 2. Head Banger

A figure that appears at the **reception window** and repeatedly bangs its head against the glass.

- **Danger Level:** 🟢 Low
- **How to handle:** **Close the shutter** while the Head Banger is rearing back for another strike. Timing is important — close it during the backswing, not while it's making contact.
- **Tip:** This is one of the easiest hazards to deal with. Don't panic, just time the shutter close.

### 3. Skinwalker

A dangerous entity that roams the hospital **corridors**, mimicking the appearance of patients or staff. It is one of the most lethal threats in the game.

- **Danger Level:** 🔴 Very High
- **How to handle:**
  - **Shoot it with a gun** (most reliable).
  - **Use a taser** to stun it temporarily.
  - **Hold E** near it to trigger an interaction that may repel it.
- **Warning:** Do not engage in melee or try to outrun it. Use your tools.
- **Tip:** Keep your gun or taser equipped when moving through corridors, especially late in a shift.

### 4. The Stalker

A humanoid entity that **stares at you** from a distance. Making eye contact with The Stalker **costs 10 Sanity**.

- **Danger Level:** 🟡 Medium (Sanity threat)
- **How to handle:** **Avoid looking at it.** Turn away, look at the floor, or focus on your tasks while it's present. It will eventually leave.
- **Sanity cost:** -10 per stare
- **Tip:** If you notice a figure in your peripheral vision that seems to be watching you, DO NOT look directly at it.

### 5. Tendril

A fleshy tentacle that appears in the **surgery room** and grows over time. A timer starts when it spawns.

- **Danger Level:** 🟡 Medium
- **How to handle:** **Complete the surgery before the timer runs out.** If you finish the surgery in time, the Tendril disappears. If the timer expires, it attacks.
- **Tip:** Prioritize speed in the surgery room when a Tendril is present. Don't waste time on optional steps.

### 6. Camera Anomaly / Demon Cat

An anomaly that appears on your **camera feeds**, manifesting as a demonic cat-like figure. Triggering it results in a **jumpscare**.

- **Danger Level:** 🟡 Medium (Sanity threat)
- **How to handle:** **Press the Exit button** on the camera interface to leave the camera view immediately when you spot it.
- **Sanity cost:** -20 if the jumpscare triggers
- **Tip:** If something on the camera looks like a cat, Exit immediately. Don't wait to inspect it.

### 7. Don't Look Up / Flesh Ceiling

The ceiling becomes a **fleshy, organic surface** that damages you if you look up at it.

- **Danger Level:** 🟡 Medium
- **How to handle:**
  - Use **Eye Drops** to temporarily protect yourself.
  - Use the **door trick** — stand in a doorway where the ceiling is normal and look up briefly to check corridors without full exposure.
- **Tip:** Keep Eye Drops in your inventory for emergencies. This hazard punishes curiosity.

### 8. Hiders / Translucent Man

Semi-transparent entities that **hide in rooms** and can only be detected by listening for their **breathing sounds**.

- **Danger Level:** 🔴 High
- **How to handle:** Listen carefully for breathing when entering a room. If you hear it, **use your gun** to shoot the Hider before it can attack.
- **Tip:** Turn up your game audio or wear headphones — the breathing sound is subtle but distinct. It's your only warning.

### 9. Death Ritual

A patient begins **floating in the air**, surrounded by candles in a ritual formation. A timer counts down.

- **Danger Level:** 🔴 High
- **How to handle:** **Remove all the candles before the timer expires.** Interact with each candle to extinguish it. If the timer runs out, the ritual completes and the patient is lost (or worse).
- **Tip:** Move quickly between candles. The timer is tight, especially with multiple candles to extinguish.

### 10. Slime

A **slippery green substance** that appears on the floor.

- **Danger Level:** 🟢 Low
- **How to handle:** **Interact with the slime to clean it up.** It's a minor hazard but can slow you down or cause slipping.
- **Tip:** Clean slime promptly to keep pathways clear, especially in emergency situations.

### 11. Fire

Fires can **break out randomly** in the hospital.

- **Danger Level:** 🟡 Medium
- **How to handle:** Use a **fire extinguisher** to put out the fire. Fires can spread if left unchecked and may destroy equipment or block paths.
- **Tip:** Know where fire extinguishers are located before you need them. Keeping one in your inventory is ideal.

## Survival Priority Chart

When multiple threats appear simultaneously, handle them in this order:

1. **🔴 Very High** — Skinwalker (kill it first)
2. **🔴 High** — Bed Monster, Hiders, Death Ritual (deal with immediately)
3. **🟡 Medium** — The Stalker, Tendril, Camera Anomaly, Don't Look Up, Fire (manage when safe)
4. **🟢 Low** — Head Banger, Slime (handle when convenient)
