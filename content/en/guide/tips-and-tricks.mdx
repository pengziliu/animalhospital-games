# Animal Hospital Tips and Tricks

## Sanity Management

### Passive Recovery
- **Doctor/Surgeon**: Recover Sanity with every patient heal
- **Secretary**: Recover Sanity when checking in patients
- **Psychologist**: Double all Sanity effects (positive AND negative)

### Active Recovery
- **Coffee** (+5 Sanity per sip, free in lobby)
- **Chocolate** (+20 Sanity per bite, premium)
- **Psychologist + Chocolate** = 40 Sanity per bite

### Sanity Preservation
- Avoid cursed photos (-10 Sanity)
- Don't stare at the Stalker (-10 Sanity)
- Close shutters quickly to prevent anomaly encounters

## Anomaly Detection Shortcuts

### Fast Screening Protocol
1. Quick glance at patient (3-5 seconds)
2. Take photo immediately
3. Check CCTV while photo develops
4. Review photo on desk
5. If ANY doubt → close shutter

### Common Mistakes
- **Not waiting long enough** — Twitching anomalies need 4-5 seconds to manifest
- **Forgetting CCTV** — Some anomalies ONLY appear on cameras
- **Picking up cursed photos** — Costs 10 Sanity before you even realize
- **Leaving cameras broken** — Blind spots let anomalies through

## Class Synergies

### Best Solo Build
**Surgeon Level 3** — Self-sustaining Sanity + speed boost. No teammates needed.

### Best Co-op Builds
- **Player 1**: Secretary (front desk Sanity recovery)
- **Player 2**: Surgeon (treatment room)
- **Player 3**: Security/Secret Agent (anomaly elimination)

### Budget Build
**Psychologist Level 2+ + Coffee** — Doubled Sanity recovery from Coffee makes you nearly unkillable.

## Economy Tips

### Fastest Way to Earn Animal Coins
1. Play on shorter shifts for consistent coin income
2. Use Doctor/Surgeon for self-sustaining longer shifts
3. Prioritize patient throughput over perfect anomaly detection
4. Invest in "Patients Recover 25% Faster" and "50% Faster DNA Analysis"

### What NOT to Buy
- **Security class** — Taser is free in Reception Office
- **Fire Extinguisher** — Already free in shop
- **Run Fast Cola** — Paramedic class gives better version

## Audio Cues

Learn these audio cues to detect danger early:
- **Skinwalker roar** — Dangerous, prepare gun/taser
- **Hider breathing** — Listen for loud breathing near walls/windows
- **Head Banger** — Glass banging at reception window
- **Camera static** — CCTV camera is being tampered with
