# How to Play Animal Hospital — Controls & Mechanics

## Basic Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Move | WASD |
| Interact | E |
| Take Photo | Click on Camera |
| Close/Open Shutter | Click on Shutter Button |
| Use Item | Click on Inventory Item |
| Check CCTV | Click on Camera Monitor |

## The Reception Area

This is where patients arrive. You have:
- A **reception window** to view patients
- A **shutter** to block or admit patients
- A **camera** to take polaroid photos
- A **details stamper** to stamp patient information
- **CCTV monitors** to check surveillance footage

## Patient Screening Process

### Step 1: Visual Inspection
Look at the patient for 4-5 seconds. Watch for:
- Twitching or limb spasms
- Extra eyes (three eyes instead of two)
- Hollow face (empty eye sockets)
- Wide eyes and creepy smile
- Human teeth on an animal

### Step 2: Photo Check
Take a photo and wait for it to develop **on the desk**. Check for:
- Different eyes or ears than the patient
- Completely wrong animal in photo
- Static or unnatural expressions
- Cursed photos (bloodshot eyes) — **don't pick these up!**

### Step 3: CCTV Check
Check the waiting room camera for anomalies that are invisible in person:
- JPEG artifacts or censored eyes
- Unnatural body proportions
- Patient staring at camera
- Shadowy/void bodies

## Treatment Rooms

Once patients are admitted, treat them in the surgery rooms:
- **Diagnosis** — Use DNA Analysis to identify the condition
- **Surgery** — Complete the medical procedure before the timer
- **Recovery** — Wait for the patient to recover and leave

## Monsters

Monsters roam the hospital independently and can kill you:
- **Skinwalker** — The most dangerous. Emerges from admitted anomalies.
- **Bed Monster** — Hides under beds. Loves Maple Syrup.
- **The Stalker** — Tall shadowy bunny. Avoid eye contact (-10 Sanity).
- **Hiders** — Camouflaged in walls/windows. Listen for breathing.

## Upgrades and Items

The in-game shop sells upgrades using Animal Coins:
- Faster DNA Analysis
- Faster Patient Recovery
- Extra Check-In Window
- NPC Speed Boosts
- Various consumables (Coffee, Chocolate, Speed Cola)
