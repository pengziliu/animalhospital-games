# Best Builds in Animal Hospital

## Best Solo Build: Self-Sustaining Surgeon

**Class**: Surgeon (Level 3) — 2,500 Animal Coins
**Core Items**: Coffee, Taser
**Upgrades**: Faster DNA Analysis, Patients Recover Faster

This is the strongest free-to-play build. The Surgeon's passive Sanity + speed boost on every heal makes you completely self-sustaining. You can run solo shifts indefinitely once you unlock Level 3.

| Component | Choice | Why |
|-----------|--------|-----|
| Class | Surgeon Level 3 | x2 Sanity + speed on every heal |
| Primary Item | Coffee | Free Sanity recovery backup |
| Weapon | Taser (free) | Anomaly defense |
| Upgrade 1 | 50% Faster DNA | Faster diagnosis = faster throughput |
| Upgrade 2 | 25% Faster Recovery | More patients treated per shift |

## Best Budget Build: Psychologist Strategy

**Class**: Psychologist (Level 2+) — 500 Animal Coins
**Core Items**: Chocolate + Coffee
**Strategy**: Double Sanity effects make recovery items twice as effective

With Psychologist's doubled Sanity effects:
- Coffee: 5 → **10 Sanity** per sip
- Chocolate: 20 → **40 Sanity** per bite
- At Level 2: 7.5% chance to ignore Sanity loss entirely

This is the best "low investment, high return" build. 500 coins is much cheaper than Surgeon, and the synergy with consumables is incredible.

## Best Paid Build: Secret Agent

**Class**: Secret Agent (Level 3) — Robux
**Core Items**: Chocolate, Extra Taser
**Upgrades**: All speed/recovery upgrades

The Gun is the most powerful tool in the game. At Level 3 with regenerating rounds, you never run out. Combine with Chocolate for emergency Sanity recovery and you can handle any situation solo.
